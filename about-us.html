About

Only gibe the best to your palate

DINNER Restaurant, in the heart of the Costa del Sol, is part of the Rubén Mijares family, where we unite experience, passion for gastronomy and love for hospitality. We are defined by the closeness, the vocation of service, our seafood cuisine emphasizing our rice and fish. At DINNER Restaurant you will feel at home…

It's not just us who say this! Many of our guests become part of the family over time, coming from anywhere in the world. We share some examples of our Google reviews! We invite you to come to DINNER Restaurant, to enjoy a unique setting and fantastic cuisine.
